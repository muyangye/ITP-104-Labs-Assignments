iPhone 8 Plus is better than iPhone X if you are not a person who takes a lot of photos! Although it has been over 3 years since its launch, it is still worth buying!

Here are the reasons:

  • The A11 processor is still efficient enough to quickly process all Apps, including most large scale video games. Its Geekbench score is higher than iPhone X's.
  • It is the last good looking iPhone! Generations after iPhone 8 Plus are ugly because of the bangs on the top!
  • Its price is very affordable because of the time passed. It is an extremely good choice for a backup phone if you are currently using another one.
  • An iPhone without the home button is not an iPhone!
